Who We Are

Technology That
Moves You Forward

At Juneleez Tech, we believe technology should empower — not complicate. Founded in Uganda in 2021, we started with one simple belief: that businesses in East Africa deserve the same quality of technology solutions as enterprises anywhere in the world.

Today we serve clients across Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, and internationally — combining Silicon Valley-grade engineering with a deep understanding of the African market, its opportunities, and its unique challenges.
